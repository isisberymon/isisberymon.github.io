the prt commute calculator

Imagine a world where the only way to get around the lovely city of Pittsburgh was by using public transit. Who would have the hardest time? Where does service need to improve? How would you fare? This is an interactive calculator that highlights disparity in transit service between demographics in an effort advocate for underserved communities as PRT redesigns their bus lines.

TIME: in progress
ROLE: research, data viz, web design
TOOLS: tableau, html/css
